Hobby #3 cooking

I like to cook. a lot.

My favorite way of cooking lately is sous vide (using a vacuum sealed bag and putting the food in to an immersion circulator. Since you can set the temp specifically and the heat is transferred to the (usually) meat through conduction, you can cook an entire cut of meat perfectly medium rare all the way through. Flop it onto the grill for a quick reverse seer when you're done and it's perfect every time.

My favorite dishes to make are
  • Tri-tip
  • Chicken
